About Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli, born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, is one of the greatest cricketers of all time. His aggressive and passionate approach to the game has earned him a place among cricketing legends. Kohli has consistently been a top run-scorer in all formats, and his leadership has inspired a new generation of cricketers. He continues to be an icon both on and off the field.
